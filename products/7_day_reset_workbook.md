## A Recovery Jumpstart Workbook

## Welcome to Your 7-Day Reset

Whether you're in early recovery, thinking about getting sober, or supporting someone who is, this seven-day program will give you practical tools, honest reflection, and a roadmap for the first critical week of transformation.

This isn't a magic cure. Recovery is hard work. But these seven days will give you momentum, clarity, and hope that a different life is possible.

**What you'll get:**
- Daily structured exercises and reflections
- Practical tools you can use immediately
- Honest guidance from someone who's been there
- A foundation for long-term recovery

**How to use this workbook:**
- Complete one day at a time (don't skip ahead)
- Write your answers honestly—no one else needs to see them
- Use the tools even when you don't feel like it
- Reach out for support when you need it

## What's Next?

**You did it. Seven days.**

But recovery doesn't end here. This is just the beginning.

**Your next steps:**

1. **Keep going:** Commit to another 7 days, then another
2. **Get support:** Join a recovery community or program
3. **Do the deeper work:** Consider therapy, especially trauma-focused therapy
4. **Build your life:** Recovery isn't just about not using—it's about creating a life you don't want to escape from
